Roll The Bones VII

October 26th-29th

About the Rally

Roll The Bones is the premeier adventure / dual sport rally in Texas. It is not a race (depending on who you ask) but it embodies the spirit of racing, pushing yourself and your motorcycle to the limit. We encourage all riders to ride to their skill level. This is a community ride, we leave in in groups and do not leave folks behind.

Road Ride

This is an all on-road route that will put you in some of the most scenic roads in Texas, this all asphalt route will not disapoint.

50/50 ride

The 50/50 route is the flagship route of the Roll The Bones rally. Riding from country road sweepers onto private cattle ranches into backwoods trail, the 50/50 route gives you a full perspective of what Texas offers an off-road capable rider.

dirt route

So you think you've got what it takes, put a fresh set of knobbies on and get ready to throw some mud. The dirt route is going to challenge even some of the most seasoned dual sport riders.
